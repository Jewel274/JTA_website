Mission Statement

Established in 1972, the Jamaica Tourism Association represents Jamaica and its beautiful tourism attractions. The Jamaica Tourism Association primary goals are to advance the growth of Jamaica's hospitality sector and to represent the interests of its members in all forums, both locally and globally. Our Jamaican destination website strives to portray all the wonderful and lovely aspects of Jamaica, including our delectable cuisine, lovely surroundings, and our lovely locals. With this website we hope to garner interest from all around the world.
